For manufacturers of smart coffee machines, the production challenge is not just scheduling — it is knowing **exactly which raw materials need to be purchased, in what quantity, and by what date**. With complex bills of materials spanning electronic components, mechanical parts, and specialized brewing elements, getting purchasing wrong means either production delays from missing parts or excess inventory from over-ordering.

This manufacturer found their solution in [Resource Manager DB](/resource-manager-db-2) — an affordable [MRP add-on](/erp-scheduling-add-on) that linked to their existing ERP system and solved the purchasing visibility problem.

## The Challenge: Purchasing Blindly

The manufacturer described their core problem directly: "The big problem we have is knowing exactly which raw material was needed to be purchased (qty and date) based on BOM, minimum quantity to order and basic costing."

This is the classic [MRP challenge](/blogs/mrp-material-requirements-planning-guide): translating a production schedule into precise material requirements. Without MRP calculations, purchasing teams either over-order (tying up cash in inventory) or under-order (causing production delays). Both are expensive.

The existing ERP system handled basic operations but lacked the integrated MRP calculations that would connect production schedules to purchasing requirements automatically.

---

## The Solution: Affordable MRP That Links to ERP

The manufacturer chose Resource Manager DB for a specific reason: "We chose Resource Manager-DB because it was the only affordable program available where we could link to our existing ERP system and have MRP calculated."

### What RMDB Delivered

**Automatic MRP calculations** — Based on production schedules, BOMs, and inventory levels, RMDB calculated exactly what needed to be purchased, when, and in what quantities.

**ERP integration** — The system linked directly to the existing ERP, pulling data and synchronizing requirements without manual data entry or duplicate systems.

**BOM-based purchasing** — Complex bills of materials with multiple levels of components were automatically exploded into purchasing requirements, accounting for minimum order quantities and basic costing.

### The Result

"The biggest benefit with using Resource Manager-DB is the increased efficiency in purchasing department." By eliminating manual calculations and spreadsheet-based guesswork, the purchasing team could focus on supplier management and cost optimization rather than figuring out what to order.

## Why MRP Add-On Is the Smart Approach

### Full MRP Systems Are Expensive

Standalone MRP systems or full ERP replacements cost tens or hundreds of thousands of dollars and take months to implement. For a manufacturer that already has an ERP handling basic operations, adding MRP as an [affordable add-on](/erp-scheduling-add-on) delivers the capability at a fraction of the cost and time.

### Purchasing Efficiency Compounds

Every dollar saved in purchasing efficiency compounds: less excess inventory, fewer stockouts, better supplier terms from accurate forecasting, and reduced expediting costs. The smart coffee machine manufacturer's experience of "increased efficiency in purchasing department" translates directly to reduced costs.

### Consumer Electronics Complexity

Smart coffee machines involve electronics, mechanical systems, and specialty components — each with different lead times, suppliers, and minimum order quantities. Manual purchasing calculations for this level of BOM complexity are error-prone. MRP handles the math accurately and consistently.
